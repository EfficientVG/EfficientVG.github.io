About EVG

A home for efficient visual generation

Efficient Visual Generation (EVG) brings together researchers and practitioners working on efficient generative models for visual and multimodal content. The series focuses on methods that make high-quality generation more practical across constrained devices, interactive applications, production systems, and large-scale deployment.

Across editions, EVG highlights architectural innovation, data-efficient learning, resource-aware inference, and deployment-ready optimization strategies for image, video, multimodal, and world-modeling systems.
